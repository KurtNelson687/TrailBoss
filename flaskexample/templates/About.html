About TrailBoss

Thank you for visiting TrailBoss! The mission of TrailBoss is to connect outdoor enthusiasts to trails. When traveling or in unfamiliar areas, it can be nearly impossible for hikers, mountain bikers, and trail runners to find routes matching their preferences. By applying a mix of supervised and unsupervised machine learning techniques, TrailBoss fixes this problem by identifying routes that match user defined trail characteristics.

As a proof of concept, the current version of TrailBoss can find mountain bike trails in Santa Cruz, CA. However, the functionality will be extended by including additional locations and activities such as hiking, trail running, and cross-country skiing. The list of available user defined trail preferences will also be extended.

I built TrailBoss as a Data Science Fellow at Insight Data Science. Feel free to contact me if you have additional questions or want to chat data!
