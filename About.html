About TrailBoss

Thank you for visiting TrailBoss! The mission of TrailBoss is to connect outdoor enthusiast to trails. When traveling or in unfamiliar areas, it can be nearly impossible for hikers, mountain bikers, and trail runs to find routes that match their preferences. By applying a mix of supervised and unsupervised machine learning techniques, TrailBoss fixes this problem by identifying routes that match user defined trail characteristics.

As a proof of concept, the current version of TrailBoss can be used to find mountain bike trails in Santa Cruz, CA. However, the functionality will be extended by including additional locations and activities such as hiking, trail running, and skiing. The list of availble user defined trail preferences will also be extended.
